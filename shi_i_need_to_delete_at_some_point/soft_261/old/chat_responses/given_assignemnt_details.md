#### **3. Give Feedback (Big Part)**

For **2 presentations**, write feedback on:

* **Visual design of slides**
* **Presentation delivery**

Requirements:

* **24 total comments** (6 per prompt × 2 prompts × 2 teams)
* Must be:

    * Specific
    * Actionable
    * Significant
    * Balanced (good + bad)
    * Professionally written

Submit as a **PDF**

---

#### **4. Create a Visualization**

* Pick **1 project**
* Make a **new, original visualization** (not reused or simple like a table)
* Must:

    * Follow visual design principles
    * Fit into their slide deck
    * Be clear and meaningful (not trivial)
* Include:

    * Legend (if needed)
    * Note saying where it goes in slides

Graded on design quality across multiple visual principles

Submit as a **slide-sized PDF**

---

#### **5. Justify Your Design (≤300 words)**

Explain:

* What your visualization communicates
* Why a visualization is better than text/table
* How it improves the original presentation
* What design principles you used

Submit as a **PDF**

### **Bottom Line**

You:

1. **Watch presentations**
2. **Critique them deeply**
3. **Design a better visual**
4. **Explain why it’s better**
