   1. what went well 
      1. Speaker technical presentation skills

   2. what needs improvement
      1. The speaker went over the stakeholders in this project too quickly
         1. The audience was not able to identify what roles each project member held. 
            1. They way you fix this is to slow down on explaining on who does what more effectively. You state the name of the project member, then the role title, then what the role title does. 
      2. Award acrimony transition 
         1. for those not in the know, it's a tradition among the UNL senior design event to give your sponsor, who is usually a professor, a award for their help during the project. This ritual happens for every senior design showcase most times the way this transition happens is very awkward because the way the speaker announces it is not punctual in a way where it gains momentum or excitement from either the audience or the project members. Because of that, the ritual is very awkward.
            1. the way you fix this is by gradually decreasing the pace at which you speak your words, and increasing the enthusiasm in your voice as you conclude with your request that the sponsor receive the award.
